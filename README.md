# Let Maryland Vote

**Ballot Initiative Rights for the People of Maryland**

## Our case in a nutshell

Maryland is one of the few states where citizens **do not have the right to initiate legislation or constitutional amendments by ballot**.

This project exists to change that.

Ballot initiative rights give citizens a lawful, peaceful mechanism to place issues directly before voters when the legislature refuses to act. They do not replace representative government. They correct for its failure.

Let Maryland Vote is a focused effort to restore that missing democratic tool.

---

## What this project is

* An effort to **establish citizen-initiated ballot access** in Maryland
* A nonpartisan, structural reform initiative
* A prerequisite reform that enables future voter-driven policy debates
* A public, transparent project developed in the open

This project is about *process*, not outcomes.

---

## What this project is not

To avoid confusion and bad-faith framing, this project explicitly is **not**:

* A political campaign or candidate vehicle
* A partisan effort
* A specific policy initiative (taxes, zoning, education, etc.)
* A ZIP Code apportionment proposal
* A substitute for the legislature

Ballot initiative rights are the **mechanism**. What voters choose to do with that mechanism comes later and belongs to them.

---

## Why ballot initiative rights matter

Without ballot initiative rights:

* Citizens are fully dependent on legislative goodwill
* Structural reforms that reduce legislative power are functionally impossible
* Issues with broad public support but weak institutional backing stall indefinitely

Ballot access restores leverage to the people while preserving constitutional order.

---

## Strategy and approach

This project is intentionally incremental and realistic:

1. **Public education and awareness**
2. **Non-binding resolutions** at the municipal and county level
3. **Legal and constitutional analysis**
4. **Coalition building across ideological lines**
5. **Pressure for a constitutional amendment enabling ballot initiatives**

No shortcuts. No theatrics. No illusions about difficulty.
